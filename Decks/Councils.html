Disregarding dissent?

Routine decisions by simple majority:

  • Large minorities routinely dissatisfied
  • Still not be bound by the outcome
  • Might leave

Finding a Solution

Formal Objection = lack of consensus = natural blocker

An FO handling mechanism enables principled decisions acceptable to the community despite no consensus.

NOT unbiased decision about right vs wrong, guilty vs innocent.
‚Üí Informed and principled decision for a best way forward.

Recusals Based on Conflict of Interest?

  • Subject to pressure and intimidation
  • Conflicts of interest are subjective:
    • Nobody on the AB/TAG/Team is indifferent about the W3C or the Web
    • AC Reps, editors, chairs: collective or individual views?
    • Agreeing with someone: conflict of interest?
    • Having known views: conflict of interest?
  • Each person judged on different criteria: their own
    ‚Üí Would exclude people who care about conflicts of interests as much as than people who have them

Automatic Exclusion?

Predefined strict rules are easily worked around

Potential objectors on the AB or TAG could find someone to proxy their Objection

Dismissal

  • After discovery and disclosure
  • Everybody is judged by the same (collective) criteria, no bias against the principled
  • Allows for removing deeply problematic cases, while generally preserving breadth, diversity, and electoral representivity
